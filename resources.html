Noli Me Tangere: A Novel

José Rizal; translated by Soledad Lacson-Locsin. Noli Me Tangere is Latin meaning "touch me not." In this modern classic of Filipino literature, José Rizal exposes "matters. . . so delicate that no one can touch them," unveiling an epic history of the Philippines that became the country's most significant political book in the nineteenth and twentieth centuries.
