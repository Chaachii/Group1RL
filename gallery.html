Birthplace and Family

Born in Calamba, Laguna as the seventh child of Don Francisco Mercado II and Teodora Alonso Realonda

Baptism

Baptized in Calamba Catholic Church by Rev. Rufino Collantes. The original record was lost in a church fire.

Early Education

At age 3, learned the alphabet from his mother Teodora, who became his first teacher

Ateneo Municipal de Manila

Began his secondary education here at age 11

University of Santo Tomas

Pursued his medical studies before heading to Europe

Universidad Central de Madrid

Studied Philosophy, Letters, and Medicine

Noli Me Tangere (1887)

His first novel, published during the Spanish colonial period

El Filibusterismo

His second novel, serving as a sequel to Noli Me Tangere

Martyrdom

Executed by firing squad on December 30, 1896 in Manila
